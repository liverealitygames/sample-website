Frequently Asked Questions

Live Reality Games (LRGs) are games that typically emulate a reality TV show format that last for at least a day where players compete in a variety of physical and social challenges to win.

There are a lot of LRGs out there in the world, but there is no good resource that brings them all together in one place and accessible to people. This spreadsheet aims to change that.
